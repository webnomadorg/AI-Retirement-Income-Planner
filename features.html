Comprehensive by design

Everything the planner can do

Transparency you can click into

Behind every figure is a reason. Click any badge or info dot to see exactly why a number is what it is — the bracket it fell into, the rule that applied, the assumption behind it. Confidence comes from understanding, not from being told to trust the math.

  • Instant clarity for every figure
  • Understand the impact of every decision
  • Make confident, informed choices
Built‑in education

Plain‑English explanations sit beside every result. A searchable A–Z glossary, concept primers, contextual help and curated tutorial videos turn confusing acronyms — IRMAA, ACA, RMD, NIIT — into things you actually understand.

  • Glossary, guides & videos built in
  • Understand the “why” behind every result
  • Financial literacy that builds confidence

The complete set

Eight capability areas, one file

Conditional features appear only when relevant to your currency, residency and filing status — so the tool stays clean.

Core modeling

  • Five‑phase retirement timeline with user‑defined boundary ages
  • Month‑by‑month simulation of balances, withdrawals & income
  • Per‑account drawdown — 401k, cash, taxable equity, Roth, Super
  • Cost‑basis‑aware equity sales and one‑time lump events
  • Replan‑from‑today to fast‑forward an in‑progress retirement

Income streams

  • US Social Security with FRA by birth year, claiming age & COLA
  • Spouse Social Security with age‑gap timing & survivor handling
  • US pension / disability (two streams; taxable or tax‑free)
  • UK State Pension (Triple Lock), Canada CPP/OAS, Australia Age Pension
  • Rental, annuity, passive income & part‑time work

Tax engine

  • US federal brackets, deductions & senior add‑on, inflation‑adjusted per phase
  • Social Security provisional‑income taxation, the IRS way
  • NIIT, IRMAA (2‑year lookback) & flat state tax with exemptions
  • UK / Canada / Australia regimes with US Foreign Tax Credit
  • Roth conversions with MAGI / ACA / IRMAA impact

Healthcare

  • ACA premium, subsidy & Silver‑CSR eligibility (pre‑Medicare)
  • Medicare Part B & D estimates from age 65
  • Overseas healthcare costs for expatriate residence
  • Separate healthcare‑specific inflation rate

Risk & analysis tools

  • Monte Carlo simulation with configurable volatility & inflation
  • Historical backtest against real US market history
  • Stress test and three‑scenario comparison
  • Social Security optimizer (claiming‑age breakeven)
  • Drawdown strategies, What‑if explorer & annuity calculator

AI assistance optional

  • AI chat & sidebar that reason over your live plan
  • Verifiable proposals — numeric, simulated & user‑approved
  • Tax‑rate research prompt to refresh current‑year figures
  • Runs under your own API key; fully optional

Output & reporting

  • Phase dashboard with per‑phase income, tax & net (nominal & real)
  • Multi‑page PDF export of the full plan
  • Plan‑health checks and confidence scoring
  • JSON import / export for backup & sharing

Experience & education

  • Multi‑currency (USD/GBP/CAD/AUD/PHP/THB) & residency modes
  • Light / dark and additional visual themes
  • Info popovers, A–Z glossary, help system & concept primers
  • Quick Setup Wizard and curated explainer videos
