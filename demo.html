What you’ll be looking at

The demo is loaded with a simple, relatable sample plan: a single filer retiring at 62 with $530,000 saved across a 401k, cash and a brokerage account, claiming $2,200/mo Social Security at 62, plus a $10,000 inheritance arriving in Phase 2. Every number, chart and health check you’ll see is the planner’s genuine output for that plan.

✅ What works — the real thing

  • All 13 tabs — phase cards, balance & income charts, tax detail
  • Confidence score with live Monte Carlo & historical backtest
  • Stress test grid, Scenarios, SS Optimizer, Drawdown strategies
  • What-if explorer — the Social Security claiming slider is fully live
  • A scripted AI chat conversation — including one proposal you can actually apply and undo to watch the whole dashboard recalculate
  • The 6-step setup wizard — open it from Help and step through the whole onboarding flow (read-only)
  • Currency display switching, themes, dark mode, text sizing, the full built-in Help

🔒 What’s disabled — it’s a sandbox

  • Editing any number — the sample plan is fixed, so the demo stays consistent
  • Saving, loading, importing or exporting plans
  • The live AI advisor (the demo chat is scripted; the full version connects to a real AI model)
  • Replan-from-today and example-plan loading

Anything disabled shows a small note when you click it — you’ll always know what’s demo and what’s product.

An honest note

The demo is a restricted sandbox, not a functional planner — you can’t plan your own retirement in it. It exists so you can judge the interface, depth and clarity for yourself before spending a penny. The full version is this exact app with everything switched on, working with your numbers.
