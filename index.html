Good to know

Questions, answered.

Is this a subscription?

No. It’s a one‑time purchase on Etsy. You download the file and own it — there are no recurring fees and no account to manage.

Is my financial data uploaded anywhere?

No. Your plan stays in your browser’s local storage unless you choose to export a backup file. Nothing is sent to a server.

Is this financial advice?

No. It is an educational planning tool that helps you model scenarios and understand your options. It does not provide personalised investment or tax advice.

Does it work for couples planning together?

Yes. Both spouses' ages, incomes and Social Security timing are modelled independently. The built‑in SS Optimizer searches all 81 claiming‑age combinations and recommends the split that maximises lifetime household income — weighted toward whichever spouse is likely to outlive the other.

Can I use it if I'm already retired?

Yes. A "Replan from today" mode lets you enter your current age and actual balances, and the planner re‑centres the projection from that point forward. It works equally well mid‑retirement as during the planning stage.

Does it support countries outside the US?

Yes. It models the US, UK, Canada and Australia — with local taxes, state pensions and healthcare built in — plus expat modes for those living elsewhere. Six currencies are supported (USD, GBP, CAD, AUD, PHP, THB), and US expats can model both home and host‑country tax regimes simultaneously.
