Your first afternoon

Getting started is the easy part

Open one file — no installation, no account, nothing to configure. Three friendly guides greet you the moment you launch it, and you can have a real, stress‑tested plan the same afternoon. Here’s exactly what your first session looks like.

You’re never dropped in cold

Three guides meet you at the door

They open automatically the very first time you launch the planner — and you can relaunch any of them from the Help menu whenever you like.

Concepts Primer

The five ideas that actually matter — MAGI, Full Retirement Age, the ACA subsidy cliff, RMDs and bracket strategy — explained in plain English before you touch a single number.

Quick Setup Wizard

Answer a handful of friendly questions and the wizard builds your first complete, end‑to‑end plan in about three minutes. Every default is yours to change later.

Feature Tour

A guided walk around the dashboard that points out what every panel does, so nothing feels mysterious and you always know where to look.

Two ways to begin

Whether you’re planning ahead or already retired

Most planners assume you haven’t retired yet. This one has a purpose‑built path for both — so you’re not forced to recreate a life you’re already living.

Planning ahead

From zero to a full plan in minutes

  • Run the Quick Setup Wizard for an instant baseline
  • Refresh the tax figures with one click so your numbers reflect current law
  • Enter your balances and the income you’ll want, phase by phase
  • Fine‑tune, then ask the built‑in AI co‑pilot to sanity‑check it
Already retired

Fast‑forward to your real situation

  1. 1
    Run the wizard with your historyUse the age you actually retired at and rough “at retirement” balances.
  2. 2
    Fetch current tax ratesOne click brings brackets, IRMAA, FPL and the standard deduction up to today’s law.
  3. 3
    Click “Replan”Enter your real current age, balances and any Social Security you already collect — the plan re‑anchors to today.
  4. 4
    Optimise from hereAsk the AI co‑pilot to flag risks and propose changes you can apply with one click.

Good to know: the AI co‑pilot is the one optional extra — it runs on your own Anthropic API key (typically a few cents per use, nothing stored or shared). Everything else — the full five‑phase engine, tax, healthcare, Monte Carlo, optimisers and reports — works completely without a key.

Experiment freely

Nothing you do is ever lost

Try a bolder retirement age. Test a Roth conversion. Compare claiming Social Security at 62 against 70. You can poke at anything without fear — the planner quietly saves every change and lets you step back at any time.

  • Autosaves to your own browser as you work — close the tab and pick up where you left off
  • Undo / redo on every change
  • Name each scenario and keep an unlimited library by exporting and re‑importing
  • Three saved‑plan slots for quick side‑by‑side comparison
