Affiliate FAQ

How are my sales tracked?

Through your unique Etsy discount code. Etsy records every order that uses it, so there are no links to manage and nothing to install.

When and how do I get paid?

Monthly, for the previous month's sales once they have cleared on Etsy. We will confirm your preferred payout method (for example PayPal) when you are accepted.

How much can I earn?

50% of the net profit on each sale made with your code. There is no cap and no cost to join.

Can I use my own code to buy?

No — codes are for your audience, not self-purchases. Sales that are refunded or that breach the terms are not eligible.

Can the terms change?

The program may evolve over time. We will give reasonable notice of any change, and you can stop taking part at any time.
